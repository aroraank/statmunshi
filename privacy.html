Section 01

Introduction & Scope

Welcome to CricIQ ("the App", "we", "us", or "our"). CricIQ is a free Android application developed and operated by CricIQ, an independent developer based in India. The App is available on the Google Play Store under the package name com.criciq.app.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the CricIQ Android application. It applies to all users of the App anywhere in the world, including users in India subject to the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021.

The App is designed specifically for cricket fans who wish to explore Indian Premier League (IPL) statistics, receive AI-powered cricket analysis, follow live scores, participate in quizzes, play the player guessing game, and use the win probability calculator. The App does not offer real-money gaming, gambling, wagering, or any financial transactions.

Our Core Commitment: CricIQ is built on the principle of minimal data handling. We do not require user accounts, do not collect personally identifiable information, and do not monetise user data in any way whatsoever.

By installing or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please uninstall and discontinue use of the App.

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

CricIQ uses AsyncStorage (React Native's local device storage mechanism, equivalent to localStorage) solely to cache IPL cricket statistics data on your device. This caching improves app performance and reduces repeated network calls for static data.

  • This data consists exclusively of cricket statistics (match scores, player stats, team records, etc.)
  • This cached data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • No personal or identifying information is included in this cached data
  • You may clear this cached data at any time by clearing the App's storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the App

2.2 AI Query Text

When you use the AI Analysis feature, the text of your cricket-related query is transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. This query text:

  • Contains only the cricket question you choose to type
  • Does not include your name, device ID, location, or any identifying information
  • Is not stored by CricIQ on any server
  • Is processed by Anthropic in accordance with their privacy policy (see Section 5)

2.3 Live Score API Requests

When you access live scores, the App makes HTTP requests to a third-party cricket data API. These requests may include technical information transmitted as part of standard HTTP protocols (such as your device's IP address visible to the API provider's servers). CricIQ does not control, log, or store this information. Refer to your chosen internet connection provider's and the API provider's privacy policies for details.

Section 03

How We Use Information

Because CricIQ does not collect personal information, there is no personal data for us to use, sell, or share. The following describes how we use the limited technical interactions that occur during App use:

  • Cricket statistics cache — stored locally on your device solely to provide faster app performance. Not accessible to us.
  • AI query text — forwarded to Anthropic's Claude API solely to generate a cricket analysis response shown to you in the App. We do not retain this text.
  • Live score requests — made to fetch real-time match data solely to display scores within the App.

We do not use any information for: advertising, profiling, marketing, user tracking, analytics, machine learning model training by us, or any purpose other than delivering the core cricket features of the App.

Section 04

Data Storage & Security

CricIQ does not operate any backend servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that stores user data. All App data resides:

  • On your device — cricket statistics cache stored in AsyncStorage, protected by your device's operating system security and any screen lock you have configured
  • Transiently in transit — AI queries and live score requests are sent over HTTPS (TLS-encrypted connections) and are not retained by us

Since we do not collect or store personal data on any server, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal information through CricIQ is effectively nil. The security of locally cached cricket statistics data depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

Internet Permission: The App requests only the INTERNET Android permission, solely to fetch live score data and call the Anthropic Claude API. No other Android permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, etc.) are requested or used.

Section 07

Data Retention

Since CricIQ does not collect or store personal data on any server operated by us, there is no personal data retention period to define.

  • Device cache (cricket stats): Retained locally on your device until you clear the App's data or uninstall the App. You control this entirely.
  • AI query text: Not retained by CricIQ. Anthropic's retention of API inputs is governed by their privacy policy and API usage terms.
  • Live score requests: Not retained by CricIQ. The respective API provider may retain standard server logs per their own policies.
